About Us

Our main goal is to provide a web-based platform that people can exchange or lend their books in order to throw them away. Thanks to that platform unused books will be used by somebody that really need it. In addition to that same book will be used by different people so that the number of tree used to get a book will be reduce. Also, people gain their money back by renting the book.

We are aiming to decrease it with an human-friendly way.
